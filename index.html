About DYsoft

Who We Are

DYsoft is an independent software development business registered in Seoul, South Korea. Founded and operated by Kim Hyung Chul, DYsoft focuses on building practical mobile tools for Korean retail investors.

Our flagship product, RSI Monitor & Simulator, is published on the Google Play Store and provides RSI-based market monitoring for KOSPI and KOSDAQ listed companies.

DYsoft is fully registered with the Korean tax authority (국세청) and holds a valid e-commerce business registration (통신판매업 신고) as required under Korean law for paid mobile applications.

Our Product

RSI Monitor & Simulator

An Android application providing real-time RSI monitoring and backtesting simulation for Korean stock market investors. Published on Google Play Store since May 2026.

01

Real-time RSI Monitoring

Top 200 KOSPI & KOSDAQ stocks by market cap, updated in real time with RSI and signal line values.

02

Buy / Sell Signal Reference

Multi-indicator analysis combining RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, and moving averages as a reference tool.

03

5-Year Backtest

RSI-based strategy simulation over 5 years of historical data. For informational purposes only.

04

Push Notifications

Real-time alerts when RSI crosses oversold (≤30) or overbought (≥70) thresholds.

05

Watchlist

Register and track personal watchlist stocks with custom RSI signal notifications.

06

Monthly Subscription

Freemium model with a KRW 2,000/month premium subscription processed via Google Play billing.
